About

I'm a full-stack developer, making a career change after years in project management

Finding a creative path has been the objective in my journey. Early on, I joined the USAF to both serve my country and pay for education. At the time, art degrees where not available online so I opted for a Bachelors in Business Adminstration: Organizational Management. Understanding the social pyschology of groups, individual motivation, and gender roles in communications will always be a fond topic of discovery and learning. But lets be real, humans are complex. Serving manageral roles from Project Coordinator, Agile Software Product Owner, and even short stint in Special Education Teaching, I still craved producing something on my own. A physical item from my hard work.

Enter the world of Full Stack Development

Dreaming up ideas for web application products are fun. But it's way more fun to make your dream a reality. In January 2022, I took the leap to complete the NuCamp's Full Stack Development Course. This is compliments previous years of self-taught experience in:

  • HTML/CSS - Microsoft MTA Web Fundamentals Certificatin
  • WordPress Development
  • C++
